Animals in the Wild

Owls

Owls are wise, nocturnal, silent, majestic birds known for their large eyes and heads.

Owls possess asymmetrical ears and tube-shaped, non mobile eyes. This allows for exceptional directional hearing and a wide field a vision by rotating their heads up to 270 degrees.
